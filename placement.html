About Training and Placement Cell

Overview


Bansal Group of Institutes (BGI) is one of the leading groups in Central India providing excellent placement opportunities to their budding professionals.
We have placed 11000+ students in over 250+ reputed National & Multinational companies ever since the institutions were established.

The Placement TeamCell is committed to providing all possible assistance to the students in their efforts to find employment and plays an integral role in
creating the illustrious placement record for students of the Bansal Group of Institutes. The experienced team with corporate background works tirelessly
towards ensuring a smooth process of employment for all students across various industries and maintains strong links with the companies. The Placement Team
further facilitates training sessions for the students to ensure their placement in the best of national and multinational companies. The main objective is to
create ample career opportunities for students in reputed Corporate companies. It also guides budding entrepreneurs in establishing successful businesses.

The Training & Placement Team of BGI ensures that the students are industry-ready before placement drives and entering the corporate world by conducting Branch
specific Technical Training, Mock Interviews, GD Sessions, Aptitude Training, and Mock Tests & Interviews by Industry experts along with Communication Skills,
Personality Development, and Company-specific training programs.
